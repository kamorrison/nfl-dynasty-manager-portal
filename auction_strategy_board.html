Best if: Elite RB/WR prices are attainable and Killanomics can win one early bidding war.

Balanced Build

Split money across multiple starting-caliber targets and stay flexible through the room.

Budget allocation: Top $43 | Mid $56 | Depth $24

Top targets: Bijan Robinson

When to pivot: Pivot if your main need tier dries up before you buy two starters.

Risk: You may lose top-end upside if elite names come in below expectation.

Upside: Most stable way to fill multiple starting needs without panic spending.

Best if: The room prices stars correctly and several WR/RB/TE options stay clustered.

Let the room overspend early, then accumulate useful starters and bench leverage in the middle tiers.

Best if: Managers with larger budgets overspend in the opening tiers.
